About this site

See our progress

This site is a bakery website that sells home-made goods like cheescake, cinnamon rolls and pastries. The themes of this website are soft tones of brown and pink. Most sweets sold are animal themed and aim to look cute. Using cute food that looks fun to eat, we promote healthy food relationships with people that may struggle in those areas. We show that food can also be something fun when eating.

Within the site we focus on informing epople on the food, and selling cute sweets.
